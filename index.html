Ceramic Coating

A permanent layer of protection.

Nano-ceramic technology bonds to your paint, creating a hydrophobic, UV-resistant shield that lasts for years — not weeks.

01

What it is

A liquid polymer that chemically bonds with your factory paint at a molecular level.

02

How it works

It cures into a hard, glass-like layer that repels water, dirt and contaminants.

03

Duration

Professional-grade coatings last 2 to 5+ years with proper maintenance.

04

Benefits

UV protection, chemical resistance, enhanced gloss, and dramatically easier washes.

Our Process

Six steps. Zero shortcuts.

01

Inspection

We assess paint condition, swirls, and problem areas before touching a panel.

02

Deep Wash

Two-bucket foam wash to safely lift dirt without introducing new scratches.

03

Paint Decontamination

Clay bar and iron removal strip embedded contaminants from the clear coat.

04

Paint Correction

Machine polishing removes swirls and restores true depth and gloss.

05

Ceramic Coating

Nano-ceramic layers are hand-applied and cured under controlled conditions.

06

Final Inspection

A panel-by-panel walk-through under LED light before handoff.
